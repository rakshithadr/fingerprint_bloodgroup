How It Works

Four steps from fingerprint to blood group

01 🖐️

Capture Fingerprint

Upload a scanned image or capture one live using your device camera or USB scanner.

02 🔍

Ridge Analysis

The CNN model extracts minutiae patterns — loops, whorls, and arches — from the ridge structure.

03 🧠

Deep Classification

MobileNetV2-based neural network classifies patterns across 8 blood groups with softmax probabilities.

04 🩸

Result + Insights

Displays predicted blood group, confidence score, donation compatibility, and probability breakdown.

BloodPrint — For research purposes only. Not a substitute for clinical blood typing.
